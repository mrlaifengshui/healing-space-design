# 行為準則 | Code of Conduct

## 中文

### 我們的承諾

作為療癒空間設計項目的貢獻者和維護者，為了促進開放和友好的社區環境，我們承諾：

- 尊重所有人，無論其年齡、身體特徵、殘疾狀況、種族、民族、性別認同和表達、經驗水平、國籍、外表、種族、宗教或性取向
- 創造一個歡迎、包容和安全的環境
- 接受建設性批評，並專注於對社區最有益的事情
- 展現同情心和尊重

### 我們的標準

#### 可接受的行為

- 使用歡迎和包容的語言
- 對不同的觀點和經驗表示尊重
- 接受建設性批評
- 專注於對社區最有益的事情
- 對其他社區成員表現出同情心

#### 不可接受的行為

- 使用帶有性暗示的語言或圖像
- 人身攻擊、嘲笑或貶低性評論
- 騷擾或跟蹤
- 發布他人的私人信息（如住址或電子郵件地址）
- 其他可能被合理認為在專業環境中不適當的行為

### 執行

社區領導者負責澄清和執行可接受行為的標準，並將對任何他們認為不適當、威脅、冒犯或有害的行為採取適當的糾正措施。

### 報告

如果您遇到不可接受的行為，請通過以下方式報告：

- 通過電子郵件聯繫項目維護者
- 在 GitHub 上提交 Issue
- 聯繫項目負責人

所有投訴將被保密審查。

### 後果

違反本行為準則的社區成員可能面臨以下後果：

1. **警告** - 對於輕微違規
2. **暫時禁止** - 對於嚴重違規
3. **永久禁止** - 對於持續或嚴重的違規

## English

### Our Commitment

As contributors and maintainers of the Healing Space Design project, in the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we commit to:

- Respecting all people regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation
- Creating a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment
- Accepting constructive criticism and focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy and respect

### Our Standards

#### Acceptable Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

#### Unacceptable Behavior

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Personal attacks, mockery, or derogatory comments
- Harassment or stalking
- Publishing others' private information (such as addresses or email addresses)
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

### Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

- Contacting project maintainers via email
- Submitting an Issue on GitHub
- Contacting the project lead

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated confidentially.

### Consequences

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:

1. **Warning** - For minor violations
2. **Temporary Ban** - For serious violations
3. **Permanent Ban** - For persistent or severe violations

**Thank you for helping us create a welcoming and inclusive community.**
